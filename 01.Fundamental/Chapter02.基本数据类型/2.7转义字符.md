# 转义字符

- 特殊的字符
- **<font color="green"> 无法 "看见" 的字符</font>**
- **<font color="green"> 与语言本身语法有冲突的字符**</font>
  
>
    \n  -   换行
    \'  -   单引号
    \t  -   横向制表符
    \n  -   换行符
    \r  -   回车
    \\  -   反斜杠 \

    >>> print('hello \n world')
    hello
     world
    >>> print('hello \\n world')
    hello \n world
    >>> print('c:\northwind\northwest')
    c:
    orthind
    orthwest
    >>> print('c:\\northwind\\northwest')
    c:\northwind\northwest
